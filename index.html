SpaceFist

SpaceFist is a 2D fighting game with a fusion of platform fighter gameplay and sumo wrestling rules. I worked as a technical game designer in a team of seven for my Game Development class at Texas A&M. I was personally responsible for designing the movesets and unique mechanics of each character and implementing the movement and knockback systems in a manner that is scalable for new moves and characters.
