Video Game Developer Portfolio

Projects

SpaceFist

SpaceFist is a 2D fighting game with a fusion of platform fighter gameplay and sumo wrestling rules. This was a semester long project for my Game Development class at Texas A&M as part of a team of seven. As a technical game designer, I was personally responsible for designing the movesets and unique mechanics of each character and implementing the movement and knockback systems in a manner that is scalable for new moves and characters.

Painta and the Slime Mix-Up

Painta and the Slime Mix-Up is a top-down shooter where players defeat enemies by shooting them with different colors to mix them into powerful effects. This game won 2nd Place Overall at the 2022 Chillennium Game Jam and was created in 48 hours for the theme of "You really shouldn't mix those!" I worked as a technical game designer in a team of four where I both pitched and designed the idea of the game to my team and implemented the enemy AI, enemy spawning logic, UI functionality and color mixing mechanics.
